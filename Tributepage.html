About the Legend

Lata Mangeshkar is an Indian playback singer and music director. She is one of the most respected and renowned playback singers in the Indian film industry. With a career spanning over seven decades, She has recorded songs in various Indian languages and has received numerous accolades for her contribution to music.

Timeline

  • 1929 - Born on 28 September 1929 Indore, India
  • 1931 - Her father, Pandit Deenanath Mangeshkar, was a classical singer and theater actor. Her mother's name is Shevanti.Lata is the eldest child of her parents. Meena, Asha, Usha and Hridaynath are her siblings in sequence.
  • 1934 - Mangeshkar took her first lessons from her father. At the age of five, she started to work as an actress in her father's musical plays (Sangeet Natak in Marathi)
  • 1943 - Her first Hindi song was "Mata Ek Sapoot Ki Duniya Badal De Tu" for the Marathi film, Gajaabhaau .
  • 2022- Died on 6 Febuary 2022

Key Achievements

  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
  • Recorded songs in more than 30 Indian languages
  • Received multiple National Film Awards for Best Female Playback Singer
  • Honored with the Dadasaheb Phalke Award

These are just a few of the many achievements and awards that she has received throughout her illustrious career. Her melodious voice and remarkable talent have left an indelible mark on the Indian music industry, and she continues to be revered as one of the greatest singers of all time.
